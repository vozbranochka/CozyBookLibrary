Non-Fiction Books

Non-fiction books are based on real facts, science, psychology, and true stories that help you understand the world better.

Sapiens

Author: Yuval Noah Harari

A deep look into the history of humankind, explaining how humans evolved and created modern society, culture, and technology.

Educated

Author: Tara Westover

A powerful memoir about a girl who grows up in a strict family without formal education and later changes her life through learning.

Outliers

Author: Malcolm Gladwell

This book explains why successful people become successful and how environment, timing, and practice shape achievement.

Thinking, Fast and Slow

Author: Daniel Kahneman

Explains how human thinking works in two systems: fast intuition and slow logical thinking, shaping all decisions we make.

A Brief History of Time

Author: Stephen Hawking

A simplified explanation of the universe, black holes, time, space, and the origin of everything around us.
