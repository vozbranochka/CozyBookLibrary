Fantasy Books

Fantasy books take you into magical worlds filled with adventures, dragons, kingdoms and heroes.

The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

A hobbit is unexpectedly taken on a journey full of danger, treasure and dragons. This is a classic fantasy story about courage and friendship.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

Author: J.K. Rowling

A boy discovers he is a wizard and enters a magical world full of secrets and danger.

The Chronicles of Narnia

Author: C.S. Lewis

Children discover a magical world where animals talk and a battle between good and evil begins.

Eragon

Author: Christopher Paolini

A young boy finds a dragon egg and becomes part of an epic battle against an evil king.

Mistborn

Author: Brandon Sanderson

A dark fantasy world where rebels fight against an immortal ruler with powerful magic systems.
